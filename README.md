# ❌⭕ Tic Tac Toe – Two Player Game

> A fun  **two-player Tic Tac Toe game** built with **js**.  
> Play with your friends locally on the same device and enjoy a classic strategy game.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- 👫 **Two Player Mode** – Play against your friends
- 🎮 **Interactive Game Board** – Click to place your X or O  
- ⚡ **Instant Win Detection** – Highlights the winner immediately  
- 🎨 **Responsive Design** – Works smoothly on desktop, tablet, and mobile  
- 🏆 **Score Tracking** – Keep track of wins for both players
